Juliana Han

Wednesday, September 27, 12:10 p.m

Ms. Han, a piantist, has been lauded not only for her thoughtful and inspired performances but also for her musical lectures which have introduced classical works to diverse audiences. Ms. Han has been a featured artist in New York's major venues, including Carnegie Hall, Alice Tully Hall, and Merkin Hall. She is a founder and director of the Piedmont Chamber Music Festival.
